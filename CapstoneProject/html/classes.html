Classes

As there is a branch of capoeira which consists of BYU-Idaho's students and another where the locals meet, the class schedules vary. Normally, BYU-I's group meets in the ballroom which is found in the STC building. This group meets twice a week, Tuesdays at 7pm and Saturdays at 10am. On the other hand, the local group meets more often. They meet on Mondays, Wednesdays, Fridays, and Saturdays. On weekdays they meet at 6pm and on the weekend, they meet at 9am. Whichever the day may be the location is always the same. It is Pioneer Park.
