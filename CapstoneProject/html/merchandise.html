Merchandise

As there is with any martial art, a uniform is required. For capoeira the uniform depends on the school which one decides to attend. The styles, colors, and school name, will obviosly vary but the clothes are the same. Along with the uniform, a student or fan may also purchase other accessories such as t-shirts which may be sold for a specific event and for that event alone, instruments, cds, and a lot more.
